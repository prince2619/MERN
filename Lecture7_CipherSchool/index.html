What is education why we need it ?

Education is a structured process of acquiring knowledge, skills, values, beliefs, and habits. It involves both formal instruction in schools, colleges, universities, and informal learning through life experiences. Education is essential for several reasons:

        Personal Development: Education helps individuals develop intellectually, emotionally, socially, and physically. It fosters critical thinking, creativity, problem-solving abilities, and self-discipline.
        
        Empowerment: Education empowers individuals by providing them with the necessary tools and knowledge to make informed decisions, participate effectively in society, and advocate for their rights.
        
        Employability: In many societies, education is closely linked to employment opportunities. It equips individuals with the skills and qualifications needed to secure meaningful employment and contribute to economic growth.
        
        Social Cohesion: Education promotes social cohesion by fostering understanding, tolerance, and respect for diversity. It helps break down barriers between different social groups and promotes equality and social justice.
        
        Personal Fulfillment: Education enables individuals to pursue their interests, passions, and aspirations. It opens up new opportunities for personal growth and fulfillment.
        
        Economic Development: Education is crucial for economic development as it enhances productivity, innovation, and entrepreneurship. A well-educated workforce is essential for a country's competitiveness in the global economy
